Tokyo Dango

Dango, also known as Takoyaki, is a popular Japanese confectionary (wagashi) that is typically made into round balls and served on a bamboo skewer. The word "dango" literally means "dumpling".

Tokyo Gyoza

Gyoza, also known as Jiaozi, are Japanese dumplings that are usually made with a thin dough wrapper and a filling of ground meat, seafood, or vegetables. It is the most popular item on our menu.

Tokyo Green Tea

About Us

Step into tomorrow with Shoryu

We empower Bangladeshi women to become entrepreneurs through education, training, and guidance. TokyoDango is our platform to foster innovation and self-reliance, creating opportunities for personal growth and contributing to Bangladesh's economic development.

How you can become a vendor

If you interested to become one of our proud vendor and change your life. You will get our full support continually. To become our vendor you need to fulfil some of our requirements. Please read them and if feel positive then proceed to next step.

Becoming an entrepreneur is no easy task. It requires strong leadership, effective management, and excellent teamwork skills. You must be capable of collaborating with others, handling pressure, and making sound decisions while breaking traditional norms. While we will provide resources, financing, and guidance, these come with expectations—you must achieve profitability within three months. Additionally, you must ensure the sustainable growth of our business, while we retain control over major decisions that you will need to execute effectively. Finally, You will need to provide at least three guarantors from your location.

That said, there’s no need to worry. We will guide, train, and prepare you to run a food cart. If you’re ready to take on this challenge, we encourage you to fill out the application form and take the first step.
